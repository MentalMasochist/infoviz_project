arXiv.org Connectivity Visualization

Welcome to the Arxiv.org Connectivity Visualization Suite!

Search and explore the entire set of arXiv.org eprints through menu inputs and visual interactions to discover trends, distributions and connections. "search arXiv" returns the intersection of all menu inputs. Subjects, Authors, and Keywords support comma-separated Boolean Full-Text Searches.

Visualizations

Submission Frequency
- Opening page shows total annual submissions.
- Search results show annual number of papers corresponding to the search.
- Menu option allows results to be normalized be total annual papers submitted.
Subject Distribution
- Opening page shows subject distribution of all of arXiv.org.
- Search result shows distribution of subjects corresponding to the search.
- Each bubble refers to a specific subject, with general subjects color coded.
- Clicking a bubble will add the subject to the search.
Author Network
- Search results show nodes of authors and edges connecting collaborators.
- Clicking an author node will add the author to the search.
Selected Papers
- Links to 10 randomly selected papers from the search results.
